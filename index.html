Michele Riechman

Hi! I have a bachelors in Exercise Science, a Doctorate in Physical Therapy, and a 200 hour yoga teacher certificate. I’m a busy mom of 4, so I get not having a lot of time to exercise, but still wanting to be in shape. I love helping other busy mom’s find the time and the how to take care of themself. I always make sure to create efficient workouts so you can get the most out of your time while also keeping the practice safe. I create my classes to incorporate strength, balance, flexibility, core work, and breathe into each practice. I like to incorporate a theme that you can learn and take with you for the rest of your week to bring more balance into your life. I offer weekly live classes for adults and kids, recorded classes, personal training, and private yoga lessons. Please reach out to me for any class requests or questions! Please join my Facebook group below for updates! My goal is to bring yoga classes and wellness knowledge to everyone in an affordable, accessible, and convenient way. I offer a variety of options including a $25 monthly membership that includes all live and recorded classes and class packs! Discounts for membership is available as part of my personal training package. Contact me at ptriechman@gmail.com to set up a private yoga or personal training session
